Bunker 42 - Soviet secret underground bunker!
At a depth of 65 meters, right under the center of the capital, there is a monument to the famous "cold war" - a huge secret bunker of the Ministry of Defense of the USSR, designed to protect 500 people in the event of a nuclear attack and support their life activity during the month.
